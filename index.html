Become part of the Roncalli community whose mission is to educate in the Catholic traditions of spiritual growth, life-long service, and academic excellence.

Following the traditions of St. John Baptist De La Salle and the Christian Brothers, as well as the heritage of the Franciscan Sisters, the Roncalli Board of Trustees is committed to providing a Christian Education for all students. The financial aid program at Roncalli makes our quality education program affordable. For more information about tuition assistance available, please see available forms.

Roncalli now participating in Scrip

While Roncalli is not starting a scrip program, St. Francis of Assisi is expanding their program to allow you to purchase scrip and use the rebates towards your Roncalli tuition. This would be for families that have completed having students at St. Francis and have students that are entering or currently enrolled at Roncalli. To participate, please read this letter and complete the form.
